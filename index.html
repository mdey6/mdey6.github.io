About Me



I am pursuing my PhD in the Electromagnetic Measurements in Communications and Computing (EMC^2) Lab at Georgia Tech, advised by Prof. Milos Prvulovic and Prof. Alenka Zajic. My research deals with identification and profiling of microarchitectural events of IoT devices through their side-channel information.

I received my Bachelor in Engineering in Electronics Engineering from University of Mumbai, India. During my time as an undergrad, I worked on several projects based on Embedded Systems and Computer Architecture.

During my free time, I like to play - both sports and board games, and travel - whenever and wherever possible.
